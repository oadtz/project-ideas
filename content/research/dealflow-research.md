# DealFlow.ai - AI Invoice Financing

## One-liner
Thai SMEs upload invoices → AI assesses credit risk in seconds → get paid in 24 hours instead of 90 days.

## Why This Sparks Joy
Thai SMEs wait 60-120 days for payment. 40% fail due to cash flow, not profitability. Traditional invoice financing takes 2-4 weeks and requires collateral. DealFlow.ai uses AI to assess invoice quality + buyer creditworthiness → instant approval. First-mover in Thai AI-powered factoring. Connect to FlowAccount, Elephant, and local ERPs.
