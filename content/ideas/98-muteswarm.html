AI agents make mistakes. A sales follow-up agent that sends the wrong message to a VIP client = relationship damage. Unlike SaaS (which just shows data), agents TAKE ACTIONS. Error cost is higher. Mitigation: Human-in-the-loop approval for high-stakes actions. Quality scoring. Gradual autonomy increase as trust builds.

MED

Thai SME Willingness to Pay
