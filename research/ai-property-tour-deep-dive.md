## 6. Regulatory Considerations

### 6.1 AI-Generated Drone Footage

- **No specific Thai regulation** against AI-generated property visuals
- Thailand's drone regulations (CAAT) apply to PHYSICAL drones â€” AI-generated footage is not regulated
- **No disclosure requirements** for AI-generated marketing content in Thailand (as of 2026)
- However: Consumer Protection Act could apply if misleading (e.g., showing views that don't exist)

### 6.2 Risk Factors

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-----------|
| "Fake" perception â€” buyers feel deceived | MEDIUM | Label as "AI visualization" + disclaimer |

| Developer projects â€” pre-construction | LOW | Rendering is already expected/accepted |
| Copyright of AI-generated content | LOW | Platform owns output, standard ToS |

### 6.3 Recommendation
